Massage

Relax massage is a gentle, pleasant hand massage that achieves relaxation of the body by combining the flow of energy. It aims to nurture the body in order to raise the general state of health. It serves to prevent stress and fatigue, as well as muscle relaxation. The accelerated pace of life causes the inevitable accumulation of stress hormones (cortisol) in the body. Increased levels of this hormone can lead to insomnia, headaches, and even digestive problems. Relax massage causes a good mood, encourages relaxation and is considered an initial step in helping the body get rid of stress hormones. You can do a relax massage of the whole body or a partial relax massage of certain parts of the body (legs, feet, back, arms, abdomen, chest, neck and face). Our suggestion is to feel the magic of a full body massage. This way you will feel rested and relieved, your muscles will be relaxed. In addition, our therapists will notice if any part of your body is under greater tension or injury, so you will know which massages are best to choose in the future.
